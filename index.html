10 — Brand Tone

Brand Tone Guide

How Novig sounds across every touchpoint. These four principles define our voice — use them as a guide for any public-facing communication.

1
Disruptive

We're not afraid to say what everyone in sports betting already knows but won't say out loud: the house has been rigging the game. Novig calls it out — directly, confidently, and without pulling punches. Our tone is disruptive because we're building something that genuinely challenges a broken system.

✓ Do
Challenge the status quo. Take a stance. Name the problem plainly.
✗ Don't
Be mean-spirited, punch down, or disrupt for its own sake. The disruption comes from truth, not attitude.
2
Transparent

Novig has nothing to hide — and that's the point. No hidden fees, no limiting or banning winners. Every order is visible in the book, every price is set by the market, and every user plays by the same rules. Our tone reflects this openness: we say what we mean, show our math, and never obscure the things other platforms would rather you not notice.

✓ Do
Be direct about what makes Novig different. Name the things sportsbooks hide — and show exactly why Novig doesn't.
✗ Don't
Signal exclusivity, assume a particular kind of fan or bettor, or make the brand feel like a club with membership requirements.
3
Make the Complex Accessible

The exchange model, vig, juice, market efficiency — these concepts are genuinely important and genuinely confusing. Our job is to make them feel obvious, not overwhelming. We don't dumb things down; we light them up. If you want in, you're in — no matter your bankroll, your background, or how many times you've won.

✓ Do
Use analogies, plain language, and concrete examples. Make them feel smart for choosing Novig. Explain, don't lecture.
✗ Don't
Assume knowledge, use jargon without context, or make casual bettors feel like outsiders.
4
For Sports Fans, By Sports Fans

Novig is the smarter alternative — a platform built entirely around sports, by people who live and breathe it. Unlike financial platforms that treat sports as a side feature, Novig is sports-native: every product decision, every market, every feature exists because of the game. We earn our place not by shouting louder, but by being right. The tone is confident and grounded — we let the math and the model do the talking.

✓ Do
Lead with evidence. Be specific about advantages (lower fees, better prices, transparency). Let the product speak.
✗ Don't
Over-claim, rely on hype, or make vague "better" statements without backing them up.
11 — The Audience

The Audience

Where traditional sportsbooks market hype and luck, Novig attracts competitors who want a fair arena where insight actually matters. Novig exists for people who see sports as something to be understood, not just watched.

1
The Sharp

Probability-driven competitors who treat sports markets like trading. They track line movement, model outcomes, and hunt for inefficiencies others miss. Most platforms limit or ban them for winning. Novig gives them what the industry rarely does: a market where strong reads can operate freely.

What they want
No limits. No bans. A transparent order book. A market that rewards insight over luck.
2
Recreational User

Everyday sports fans who watch games and enjoy having some skin in the game. They range from the casual fan who bets a few times a season, to the weekly bettor who follows their favorite team or league. They're not running models — they're acting on fandom, gut feel, and a general sense of how a matchup will go. They want something simple, fair, and fun.

What they want
An easy way to bet on the games they already watch — without hidden fees, confusing odds, or the feeling that the house is always working against them.

13 — Types of Trades

Types of Trades

Novig supports the following trade types. Use these terms consistently when referencing Novig's product offering in any public-facing communications.

01
Straights
A single trade on one outcome — the most straightforward way to play on Novig. Includes moneyline, spreads, and totals (over/under).
02
Player Props
Trade on individual player performance metrics within a game.
03
Parlays
Combine multiple trades into one — all selections must win for the parlay to pay out.
04
Futures
Trade on long-term outcomes like season champions, award winners, and more.
14 — Two-Sided Marketplace

The Two-Sided Marketplace

Novig operates as a true two-sided market — giving users the freedom to find the best available odds or create their own. Browse the live market to take an existing offer, or set your own terms and wait for another user to accept.

T
Placing a Take Order
Accepting an offer someone has already proposed. Quick, straightforward, and ready to go.
M
Placing A Make Order
You set the odds and price for another Novig user to accept. Name your terms, put them in the market, and wait for a match.
