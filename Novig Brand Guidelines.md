## Logo

Novig's logo system consists of three elements that can be used independently or in combination. **All external partners must consult with Novig to determine the appropriate logo, wordmark, or combination for their specific use case.**

### Logo Mark

The Novig icon is a stylized "N" with a dot. Available in three colorways:

| Colorway | Usage |
|---|---|
| Blue | Primary — use on white or light backgrounds |
| Black | Use on white or light backgrounds |
| White | Use on dark or black backgrounds |

### Logo + Wordmark

The icon paired with the "NOVIG" wordmark. Available in three colorways:

| Colorway | Usage |
|---|---|
| Blue | Primary — use on white or light backgrounds |
| Black | Use on white or light backgrounds |
| White | Use on dark or black backgrounds |

### Wordmark Only

The "NOVIG" wordmark without the icon. Available in three colorways:

| Colorway | Usage |
|---|---|
| Blue | Primary — use on white or light backgrounds |
| Black | Use on white or light backgrounds |
| White | Use on dark or black backgrounds |

### Logo Usage Rules

- Always consult Novig before selecting which logo asset to use
- Do not alter, distort, recolor, or modify any logo assets
- Do not use the logo on backgrounds that reduce legibility
- Maintain adequate clear space around the logo at all times
- Do not recreate the logo in any other typeface or style
- Do not add effects, shadows, or outlines to the logo

**Brand Blue — #179BE7** is the primary brand color. Use it for key brand moments, CTAs, and highlights.

**White — #FFFFFF** is used for light backgrounds and reversed-text applications.

Do not use any colors outside of this approved palette in Novig-branded materials without prior approval.

## Typography

Novig's brand typeface is **ABC Monument Grotesk** — a modern, rounded sans-serif that reflects the brand's clean and confident identity.

| Weight | Usage |
|---|---|
| Regular | Body copy, captions, supporting text |
| Medium | Subheadings, labels, UI elements |
| Bold | Headlines, emphasis, key messaging |

### Typography Rules

- Always use ABC Monument Grotesk for all Novig-branded communications
- Do not substitute with other typefaces without approval from Novig
- Maintain proper hierarchy using the weights outlined above
- Do not condense, stretch, or otherwise distort the typeface

## Language & Terminology

Novig operates as a sweepstakes. To ensure consistent and compliant communication, all external partners must use the following approved terminology in any public-facing communications about Novig. This applies to all written and verbal communications including social media, advertising, press, and promotional materials.

| ❌ Do Not Use | ✅ Use Instead |
|---|---|
| Deposit | Purchase |
| Bet / Wager | Play / Trade |
| Withdraw / Withdrawals | Redeem / Redemptions |
| Bettors / Patrons | Players / Users / Participants |
| Responsible Gaming | Responsible Gameplay |
